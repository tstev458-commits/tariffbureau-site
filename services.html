Services for Importers

Importers face cost, documentation, and compliance risk when product details are incomplete or tariff exposure is misunderstood. Tariff Bureau supports importers with structured review before shipment, purchasing, or supplier commitment.

Tariff Classification Review

Review product descriptions, materials, use cases, and technical details to support tariff classification planning and HTS code review.

HTS Code and Duty Exposure Support

Evaluate potential duty exposure and identify classification questions that may affect landed cost, pricing, and sourcing decisions.

Import Documentation Review

Review commercial invoice details, product descriptions, declared origin, and related import documentation for practical compliance risk.

Tariff Risk Assessment

Identify tariff-related risk before goods move through the supply chain, especially when sourcing from new suppliers, countries, or product categories.

Services for Manufacturers

Manufacturers need clarity when materials, components, assembly steps, or production locations affect country-of-origin analysis and tariff planning. Tariff Bureau helps manufacturing teams evaluate these factors before procurement or production decisions are locked in.

Country of Origin Review

Assess sourcing, manufacturing location, assembly steps, component inputs, and origin-related considerations that may affect import compliance and tariff treatment.

Supply Chain and Sourcing Risk Review

Evaluate how supplier changes, country changes, or manufacturing process changes may affect tariff exposure and import compliance risk.

Product and Component Review

Review product composition, bill-of-material style details, and component sourcing information to support practical tariff and origin analysis.

Pre-Procurement Advisory

Support business teams before committing to suppliers, manufacturing locations, purchase orders, or large-volume import decisions.
